This is actually only a special case of convex optimisation which is used absolutely everywhere (most of the time unknowingly) (portfolio optimisation, factory production, kullback-leibler divergence, statistical mechanics, etc...) As such, convex optimisation is one of the big tool one should master, like geometry, or analysis.
