What am I reading now

Convex optimization
A renaissance man ought to know that stuff.
This course really is foundational. You have probably are familiar with things like least square regression, and how it appears in many contexts. This is actually only a special case of convex optimisation which is used absolutely everywhere (most of the time unknowingly) (portfolio optimisation, factory production, kullback-leibler divergence, statistical mechanics, etc...) As such, convex optimisation is one of the big tool one should master, like geometry, or analysis.
